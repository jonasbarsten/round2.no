Dedicated to rediscovering, restoring and reissuing the Norwegian classic albums on vinyl, both the obscure and the well known.



Round 2 is a reissue-label founded by Norwegian record store Big Dipper and leading independent label Jansen Plateproduksjon.

We aim to licence and re-release the best Norwegian albums — both the obscure and the more well known. Albums that were never released on vinyl in the first place, or in very limited quantities, stuff that’s been forgotten. Hopefully our releases will be discovered by music lovers and collectors outside of Norway. We really think they deserve it!
